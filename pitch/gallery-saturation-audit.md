## Primary Prize Saturation

- Primary prize / track: Special - Walrus.
- Top three same-prize threats: generic agent memory, Walrus file manager, and checkpoint recovery dashboard.
- Winning archetype already visible: durable memory plus inspectable proof.
- Sponsor proof bar set by the strongest competitor: Walrus must be on the write/read path and visible in the result.
- Our stronger mutation: the judge kills the predecessor, scopes the will, and watches a cold successor continue with only allowed memory.
- Why a judge would choose us over the closest same-prize project: the first action is personal and replayable, while the Walrus proof remains inspectable.

## Saturation Decision

- Selected idea family: agent memory succession.
- Saturation risk: medium.
- Required mutation: keep `Kill Agent` and cold successor restore as the first-minute product beat.
- Kill condition: if the demo becomes a proof/log/report page.
- Proceed condition: the public path writes to Walrus, restores from Walrus, shows redactions, and reopens the receipt.
