# Video production contract

This is the HyperFrames G6 contract, not the final video.

## Source truth

- Product: Memory Will.
- Delivery mode: combined pitch plus product demo.
- Target duration: 270 seconds.
- Required source before rendering: public app recording, measured narration, real browser capture, and proof that the working loop exists.

## HyperFrames direction

- Composition format: HyperFrames HTML with `data-composition-id`, `data-start`, `data-duration`, and registered GSAP timelines.
- Rhythm: hook-hold-proof-demo-hold. The peak beat is the agent death and cold successor restore.
- Frame world: light forensic workbench with strong structural rules, large text, visible moss/danger accents, and product UI capture as the midground.
- Transitions: CSS crossfades for explanation, one shader or iris-style transition for the death-to-restore beat after the real app recording exists.
- Typography: large readable title frames, body text above 28px, labels above 18px, tabular numbers for proof fields.
- No silent fallback: narration and demo recording must be measured before final render.

## Blockers before final render

- Do not render a polished video until G4 real loop and G5 public smoke pass.
- Do not use screenshot slideshows as the demo.
- Do not hard-code timing around unmeasured narration.
- Do not claim Walrus/MemWal success unless the product records a real pointer or honest degraded state.
