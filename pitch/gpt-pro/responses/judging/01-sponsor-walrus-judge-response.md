The slate says the judge kills an agent, chooses a successor role, and the successor can continue only by opening scoped Walrus/MemWal memory; the artifact is a Memory Will with delegated memory list, redactions, successor replay, and compare mode. 

idea_tournament

Walrus-removable risk: Medium-low. If the “will” is just a local summary passed between two prompts, Walrus becomes removable. If the successor is genuinely cold and can only continue through scoped durable memory, Walrus is central.
